Last of Us
Poster Design

The title, "The Last of Us," is emblazoned across the top, presented in gritty, weathered font, mirroring the worn and torn nature of the world they inhabit.

Brief:The "Last of Us" movie poster encapsulates the haunting, post-apocalyptic essence of the acclaimed video game series. Set against a desolate backdrop, it features the protagonists, Joel and Ellie, silhouetted against a backdrop of ruins. The muted color palette evokes a world reclaimed by nature, with overgrown vegetation hinting at the passage of time.

Tools: Photoshop



Post-Apocalyptic Ambiance: A desolate setting dominated by ruins and overgrown vegetation, symbolizing a world reclaimed by nature after humanity's downfall.

Protagonists' Silhouettes : Joel, weathered and resolute, stands protectively beside Ellie, a young girl exuding determination beyond her years.

Cityscape of Decay: Crumbling skyscrapers and remnants of a once-thriving city serve as a haunting backdrop, reflecting the harshness of their reality.

Title "The Last of Us": Weathered typography, worn and rugged, symbolizing resilience and endurance amid chaos and destruction.

Emotional Depth: The poster conveys a story of survival, resilience, and the enduring human spirit amidst adversity, inviting viewers into a narrative of unyielding hope amid ruins. brand recognition and association.
